The process

Simple.

See full process

No confusing tech. No back-and-forth with developers who speak jargon. Just a clean, honest process from first call to launch day — handled entirely by us.

Full process breakdown →
01

We talk story

A relaxed 20-minute call — no preparation needed. We ask about your business, your customers, and what you want people to do when they find you online. You don't need to know anything about websites. Just talk to us like you would a neighbor.

02

We build your site

We handle everything — design, copy, photos, contact forms, Google Maps, and SEO setup. Within days you'll receive a private preview link to review your site before a single thing goes live. No surprises.

03

You review and refine

Look it over on your phone and your computer. Tell us what you love, what needs changing, what we missed. We revise in plain English — no tech terms required — and keep going until you're completely satisfied.

04

Your site goes live

We connect your domain, configure hosting, secure it with HTTPS, and submit it to Google. We also set up or claim your Google Business Profile. You wake up visible, credible, and ready to receive new customers.
