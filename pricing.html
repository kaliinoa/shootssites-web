What about

Websites need a home on the internet (hosting) and an address (domain). If you're on the Care Plan, hosting is included. For Starter and Growth builds, we help you set up affordable hosting — typically around $10–20/month — on a platform you own and control.

Domains usually run $12–18/year. We'll help you register one if you don't have it already. You own everything — your domain, your hosting account, your site files.
