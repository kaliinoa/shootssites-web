The story

Why I started
ShootsSites.

Shoots. I'm Kali'inoa — a Hawaii native, Harvard graduate studying economics, and the founder of ShootsSites.

Growing up in Hawaii, I understood early on that there are two worlds operating at once. There's the surface — the beaches, the tourism, the postcard version — and then there's the world underneath: the tradespeople and small business owners who keep the islands actually running. The plumbers, electricians, roofers, and landscapers who take real pride in their craft and their community, but rarely get the recognition — or the customers — they deserve.

Studying economics at Harvard deepened my understanding of why some businesses thrive while others stay invisible — and what it actually takes to grow and scale. I became increasingly focused on the gap between quality and visibility. Businesses doing exceptional work that simply weren't being found.

Website development was never the destination. It was the vehicle. I got into it to get closer to business owners — to hear how they think about growth, understand their challenges firsthand, and find ways to genuinely help them scale. Building websites for Hawaii's trades community sits at the intersection of everything I care about: economics, small business, and real impact on the people and places that shaped me.

Every client I work with is a lesson. Every site I build is an investment in the community that raised me.

— Kali'inoa, Founder

Why us

What makes us
different.

We know Hawaii

We're not a mainland agency that added "Hawaii" to a search campaign. We live here. We understand the islands, the culture, the communities — and we build websites that feel like they belong here, not copy-pasted from a template farm.

We specialize in trades

A plumber's website has different needs than a restaurant's. We've thought deeply about what converts for tradespeople — prominent phone numbers, clear service lists, trust signals, review sections — and we build that in by default.

We handle everything

Domain, hosting, security, Google listing, analytics — you don't have to figure out any of it. Hand us the project and we take it across the finish line. You focus on the work that actually makes you money.

Real support after launch

Most agencies disappear after they hand over the site. We stick around. Whether through our Care Plan or just an email when something looks off, we stay in your corner after launch day.

No long-term contracts

The Care Plan is month-to-month. If your situation changes, you're never locked in. We earn your business every month by being genuinely useful, not by holding your site hostage.

You own everything

Your domain, your content, your code — it's all yours. If you ever want to take your site elsewhere (we hope you won't), you can. We believe in building trust, not dependency.
