Step by step

From first call
to live site.

01

We talk story

A relaxed 20-minute call — no preparation needed on your end. We'll ask about your business: what you do, which island you're on, who your ideal customers are, and what you want them to do when they find your site.

This call is free and there's no obligation to proceed. We just want to understand your business before we quote anything.

What we cover

  • Your trade and service area
  • Who your customers are
  • What makes you different from the competition
  • Whether you have photos or need us to source them
  • Your domain and hosting situation
  • Which package fits your needs and budget
02

We send a clear quote

After the call, we send you a written quote — the exact pages we'll build, every feature included, the timeline, and the price. No vague estimates, no "it depends" pricing.

If you want to adjust the scope (add a gallery, drop a page), we'll update the quote and send a new version. When you're happy, you give us the green light and we start building.

What the quote includes

  • Full list of pages and features
  • Flat, fixed price — no hourly surprises
  • Clear launch timeline
  • What we need from you to start
  • Care Plan option if relevant
03

We build your site

Once you've approved the quote, we get to work. We design and build your site based on everything we learned in the call — your trade, your customers, your personality. We write the copy, source the photos, and put it all together.

For Starter sites this typically takes 3–5 days. Growth sites take 5–7 days. When it's ready, we send you a private preview link.

What we handle

  • Design — layout, colors, typography
  • Copywriting — clear, professional text
  • Photo sourcing if needed
  • Contact form setup
  • Google Maps integration
  • Mobile responsiveness
  • Basic SEO (titles, descriptions, keywords)
04

You review and refine

You get a private preview link. Look through the whole site on your phone and your computer. Tell us what you love, what you want changed, and what we missed. We speak plain English — no need to describe things in tech terms.

Starter plans include 3 rounds of revisions. In practice, most sites are finalised in 1–2 rounds. We stay at it until you're completely happy.

Common revision requests

  • Tweak the wording on a page
  • Swap in a different photo
  • Change colors or fonts
  • Add or remove a section
  • Adjust the contact form fields
  • Update service list or pricing
05

Your site goes live

Once you've signed off, we handle the technical launch: connecting your domain, configuring hosting, setting up your SSL certificate, and submitting your site to Google. We'll also help you set up or claim your Google Business Profile if you don't have one.

On launch day, you get an email from us with everything you need: your site URL, login credentials for anything you manage, and a quick overview of how to make basic updates yourself if you ever want to.

What we take care of at launch

  • Domain connection and DNS setup
  • SSL certificate (HTTPS)
  • Hosting configuration
  • Google Search Console submission
  • Google Business Profile setup / claim
  • Final cross-device testing
  • Post-launch check-in
Before we start

What to have ready.

You don't need much — and nothing on this list is a hard requirement. But the more you can share, the faster and more personalized your site will be.

About your business

  • Business name and what you do
  • Which island(s) you serve
  • How long you've been operating
  • Any licenses or certifications
  • What makes you different

Content (if you have it)

  • Any photos of your work
  • Your business logo (or we'll make one)
  • Customer reviews you've received
  • Any existing website or social media
  • Preferred contact method

Technical (optional)

  • Your domain name (if you have one)
  • Existing hosting login (if applicable)
  • Google Business Profile login
  • Preferred email for contact form
  • Any competitor sites you like or hate
